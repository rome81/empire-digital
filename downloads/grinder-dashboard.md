## Emergency Playbook

**If revenue drops:**
1. Check: Are ads still running? Site still up?
2. Quick win: Email your list with a flash sale
3. Medium win: Launch a new low-ticket product
4. Long win: Double down on content marketing

**If you're burned out:**
1. Take 1 full day off (no guilt)
2. Automate one thing you do manually
3. Delegate or kill your lowest-ROI activity
4. Revisit your "why"
