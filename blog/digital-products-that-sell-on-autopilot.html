What Are “Autopilot” Digital Products?

Autopilot digital products are one-time or subscription-based assets that deliver value instantly. Think budget templates, AI prompts for real estate agents, or Notion workspaces for Shopify store owners. Once created, they’re uploaded to your store, and customers buy, download, and pay automatically. No inventory. No shipping. Just revenue flowing in while you sleep.
