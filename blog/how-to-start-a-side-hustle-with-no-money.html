How To Start A Side Hustle With No Money

Empire Digital · 5 min read
```html

Starting a side hustle with no money might sound impossible, but with the right strategy and tools, it’s not only possible—it’s profitable. The key is to leverage free resources, your existing skills, and digital platforms to create value without upfront costs. Here’s how to get started—and how Empire Digital products can help you along the way.

1. Identify Your Skills or Passions
Every great hustle begins with what you already know. Can you write, design, code, or manage projects? Turn that into a service. For example, if you’re good with spreadsheets, offer to create budget templates for freelancers. Empire Digital’s Budget Tracker Template is a free resource you can refine and resell for a small fee, saving you the time to build it from scratch.

2. Use Free Digital Tools
Platforms like Canva, Trello, and Google Docs are free and powerful. Combine them with Empire Digital’s AI Prompt Packs to automate content creation, design, or project management. These tools help you streamline workflows and deliver high-quality results without spending a dime.

3. Create Digital Products
Turn your expertise into downloadable goods. A beginner’s guide to social media, a Notion workspace for productivity, or a budget spreadsheet can all be sold online. Empire Digital offers pre-built templates (like their Notion Project Planner) that you can customize and brand as your own, reducing development time and effort.

4. Affiliate Marketing
Promote products you already use and earn commissions. Sign up for free affiliate programs on ClickBank or Amazon Associates. Empire Digital itself offers affiliate opportunities for its tools, so you can earn while providing value to your audience.

5. Automate and Scale
Use free automation tools like IFTTT or Zapier to handle repetitive tasks. Pair them with Empire Digital’s Workflow Automation Scripts to save hours and focus on growing your hustle.
```
