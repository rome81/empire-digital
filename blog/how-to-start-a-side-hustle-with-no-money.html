How to Start a Side Hustle with No Money

Starting a side hustle with no money might sound impossible, but it’s totally doable with the right mindset and tools. The key is to leverage free resources, your existing skills, and platforms that let you monetize with minimal upfront costs. Here’s how to get started:

1. Identify a Skill or Niche

Start by asking: What can I do with the skills I already have? Whether it’s writing, graphic design, virtual assistance, or social media management, there’s a demand for your talent. Platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, and even social media groups let you offer services for free initially to build a portfolio. For example, if you’re a freelancer, use Empire Digital’s Notion budget template to track your income and expenses without spending a dime on accounting software.

2. Leverage Free Tools and Templates

Don’t let a lack of funds hold you back from professional-looking work. Free tools like Canva, Google Workspace, and Empire Digital’s AI prompts for content creation can help you create high-quality deliverables. For instance, use their AI prompts to draft social media posts or blog content for clients. These tools save time and money while helping you scale your offerings.

3. Monetize What You Already Have

Got a hobby or passion? Turn it into a hustle. Sell crafts on Etsy, tutor on Preply, or offer freelance writing on Medium. You don’t need an expensive setup—just a laptop and internet. For example, if you’re a student, use Empire Digital’s Notion project management template to organize your tasks and client deadlines for free.

4. Build an Online Presence

Use free platforms like GitHub Pages or WordPress to create a simple website. Showcase your work, testimonials, and services. Share your journey on Reddit or Twitter to build credibility. Empire Digital’s “How to Build a Blog in 5 Minutes” guide (free on their site) can help you launch a professional-looking site in no time.

5. Scale Smartly

Reinvest early profits into low-cost upgrades like a premium Notion template or a $10 advertising budget. Focus on niches with high demand and low competition. Empire Digital’s “AI Prompt Pack for Real Estate Agents” is perfect for targeting underserved markets with minimal spend.
