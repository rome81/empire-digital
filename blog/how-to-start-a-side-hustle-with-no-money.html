How to Start a Side Hustle with No Money: Zero-Cost Strategies for Beginners

Starting a side hustle doesn’t require a large budget. With the right mindset and tools, you can turn your skills and time into income—no capital needed. Whether you’re a student, a parent, or a career switcher, here’s how to launch a money-making venture from scratch.

1. Leverage Your Skills and Time

Your skills are your most valuable asset. Offer freelance services on platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, or even social media. Can you write, design, code, or teach? Build an audience by creating free content on platforms like YouTube, TikTok, or blogs. Share tips, tutorials, or behind-the-scenes stories.
